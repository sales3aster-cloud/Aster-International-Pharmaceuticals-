About Aster

Built to reduce procurement friction and improve sourcing confidence.

Aster International Pharmaceuticals serves pharmaceutical customers through validated sourcing relationships, practical market understanding and dependable commercial support.

Who we are

We support finished dosage formulators, merchant exporters and commercial sourcing teams with APIs, excipients and intermediates sourced through manufacturing facilities assessed for quality and suitability.

Our approach is shaped by experience across raw material supply, export operations and pharmaceutical business development in India.

How we work

Our services are crafted to add value to the supply chain while reducing procurement hassle. We align customers with the most suitable manufacturing or commercial route based on quality, market needs, timing and execution practicality.

  • Assessment-led factory alignment
  • Market insight for better commercial decisions
  • Relationship-first customer support
  • Flexible execution for export-oriented supply

Mission and Values

Collaboration, growth, synergy and trust.

Mission

To build long-term trust with customers through ethics, honesty, transparency and quality-focused supply.

Vision

To create value for global pharmaceutical customers through dependable Indian sourcing networks.

Values

Ethics, honesty, transparency, long-term vision and value innovation guide every engagement.

Why “Aster”

Named after a flower associated with resilience, admiration and patience.

The name Aster is derived from the Greek word meaning “star”. Asters are known for their color, resilience and classic beauty, and historically have been associated with positive meaning, good fortune and emotional warmth.

We chose the name to reflect the kind of brand we want to build: dependable, distinctive, memorable and rooted in positive relationships.

Operating Model

Aster works as an integrated business, not a disconnected trading desk.

Management, marketing, sales, legal coordination, operations, finance, branding, R and D understanding and HR all contribute to a single delivery model built around customer outcomes.
