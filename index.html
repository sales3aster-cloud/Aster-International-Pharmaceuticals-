Overview

A clean, dependable sourcing partner for procurement and technical teams.

We help finished dosage formulators, export procurement teams and commercial buyers source ingredients from validated manufacturing facilities while reducing procurement friction and improving confidence in quality, documentation and delivery planning.

What Aster delivers

We offer Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients, excipients and intermediates with a stronger focus on export-oriented pharmaceutical procurement. Our business model is designed to add value to the supply chain, not just supply product.

  • Validated manufacturing access
  • Support for regulated and semi-regulated markets
  • Commercial options aligned to customer needs
  • Reliable communication throughout execution

How Aster supports decisions

Beyond supply, we share practical insights into the Indian bulk drug market so customers can make more informed procurement decisions across quality, pricing, logistics and timing.

  • Market insight and product availability context
  • Factory-direct and warehouse-based supply models
  • Customer support across quality, price and delivery
  • Relationship-led service for long-term sourcing cycles
