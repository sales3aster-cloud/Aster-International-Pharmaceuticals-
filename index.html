About Aster

Strategic API sourcing with market understanding and customer-first execution.

What we do

We offer Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients, excipients and intermediates for finished dosage formulators. With a stronger focus on exports to regulated and semi-regulated markets, we source APIs and bulk drugs from regulatory compatible manufacturing facilities.

Quality is maintained through assessment and validation, while costs are managed through self-assessment protocols by the respective factories. Our services are designed to add value to the supply chain and reduce procurement hassles for customers.

How we support supply

We supply bulk drugs and APIs to finished dosage formulating companies globally and share Indian bulk drug market insights, industry information and updates that help customers make better procurement decisions.

  • Validated API manufacturing facilities with relevant approvals
  • Direct procurement from manufacturing factory
  • Warehouse-based commercial supply option near Mumbai port and airport
  • Expert support around quality, price, delivery and customer service

Why "Aster"

Named after a flower that symbolizes patience, admiration and resilience.

Aster International Pharmaceuticals takes its name from the aster plant, derived from the Greek word for "star". Asters are resilient flowers known for their color range, strength and long-standing symbolism.

The flower has been associated with patience, love, daintiness and good luck. Its historic and medicinal associations reflect the kind of brand presence we want to build: positive, dependable and memorable.
